Anthropic Launches Claude Fable 5 — Then Suspends It 3 Days Later Under US Export Control Order

TechCrunch / National Law Review / Al Jazeera June 9–12, 2026 Claude & Anthropic API
On June 9, Anthropic launched Claude Fable 5 — its most capable public model, with always-on adaptive thinking, a 1M-token context window, and 128K output tokens, outperforming Claude Opus 4.8 by more than 10% on key benchmarks. Three days later, on June 12 at 5:21 PM ET, a US government export-control directive forced Anthropic to fully disable both Fable 5 and the restricted Claude Mythos 5, citing a potential jailbreak that could surface software vulnerabilities. Anthropic chose to suspend access for all customers globally rather than attempting nationality-based segmentation. This is the first time a US export-control directive has forced the takedown of a generally available frontier model. Claude Opus 4.8 remains the current fallback. Additionally, older Claude models were deprecated on June 15 — check your API version strings now.

Google · Jun 18

Google Kills Gemini CLI Today — Forced Migration to Antigravity CLI (agy) Effective June 18

Google Developers Blog / The Register / Digital Applied June 18, 2026 Agent frameworks
As of today, Gemini CLI stops serving requests for Google AI Pro, Ultra, and free-tier users. Its replacement is Antigravity CLI (agy), a closed-source Go binary that supports Agent Skills, Hooks, Subagents, and an Extensions plugin system. The new tool orchestrates multiple background agents for parallel tasks. However, the quota model changed significantly — from 1,000 requests/day to a weekly compute-based cap that heavy users report exhausting quickly, with multi-day cooldowns. There is no 1-to-1 feature parity at launch. Organisations on Gemini Code Assist Standard/Enterprise retain uninterrupted Gemini CLI access via paid API keys.
🎯 Why it matters for you: If any internal tooling, pipelines, or dev workflows at Vulcan Labs rely on Gemini CLI, they stopped working today. The quota change (weekly cap vs. daily cap) may also affect cost modelling for Gemini-based features in your app portfolio.
